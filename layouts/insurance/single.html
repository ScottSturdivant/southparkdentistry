Insurance FAQ

Common questions about dental insurance

What if I don't have insurance?

We offer a variety of payment options to make dental care accessible for those without insurance. Please contact our office to discuss available options.

How do I know what my insurance covers?

Our team will help you understand your insurance benefits. We can provide estimates for procedures and help you maximize your coverage.

Do you accept Medicaid?

Yes! We are proud to be a Medicaid provider and welcome Medicaid patients. We believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care.

Do you offer payment plans?

We work with patients to create payment arrangements that fit their budget. Please contact our office to discuss your specific situation.
